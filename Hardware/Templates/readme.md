# Templates

## General

Each PCB should be of a specific size and contain 4 M3-sized holes for mounting them to frames. The holes are on the four corners of the pcb and have a distance of 4mm to the edges of the pcb.

## 1x1
Size: 42x42mm

## 1x2
Size: 42x84mm

## 1x3
Size: 42x126

## 2x2
Size: 84x84mm

## 2x3
Size: 84x126mm
